Don’t Vote!

published: October 9th, 2008

I like when people come up with crazy shit like this one.

My answer is simple: people don’t vote because they have other things to worry about and voting is the least thing on their heads. Although, I support the idea that everyone should vote, I do not think that ads like this one have any effect whatsoever. I like them, but no effect. You should start by telling people how voting will pay their bills and fix their problems. You cannot call for higher values when most people struggle to pay the rent.

I still like the ad although I find it completely useless and not very motivational. Don’t waste resources!
